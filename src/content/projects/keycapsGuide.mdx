## Executive Summary
Wrote and maintained an iFixit guide for replacing keycaps. The guide emphasizes concise copy, readable step breakdowns, and accessible descriptions so non-expert readers can complete the task safely.

<Subhead>Project at a glance</Subhead>

- **Role:** Technical writer, editor, photographer  
- **Deliverables:** Stepwise procedure, annotated photos, accessibility-aware copy  
- **Quality:** Clear headings, consistent verbs, scannable steps, alt text for images  
- **Link:** [Live Guide on iFixit](https://www.ifixit.com/Guide/MageGee%20Key%20Caps%20Replacement/143702)

## Context & Objectives
- Provide a reliable, easy-to-follow guide for users replacing keycaps on a budget keyboard.  
- Reduce ambiguity with consistent terminology and photo angles.

## Approach
- Broke the task into atomic steps with expected tools and outcomes.
- Wrote imperative, consistent step text (“Lift…”, “Align…”, “Press…”) and added cautions where needed.
- Ensured images have descriptive alt text (what to do, not just what’s shown).

## Edit History
- **First published:** 2021-07-13  
- **Last updated:** 2025-06-13 (minor copy/photo adjustments)

## Result
- Guide published on iFixit with a complete, beginner-friendly procedure.  
- Readers can accomplish the task quickly without prior technical knowledge.
